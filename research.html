Research

My research focuses on music cognition, mental health, and neuroscience. I am interested in understanding how music influences emotional experience and cognitive processes using behavioral and physiological methods. Besides, I love to develop small web audio tools for auditory process, you can find them in my GitHub.

How Consonant and Dissonant Music affect Neural Synchrony: An EEG Study

Using ten house music tracks with almost the same bpm (123), modulated into better and worse superposition versions using Audacity. Collected subjects' tapping synchrony to the beats and EEG data, then used Temporal Response Function to test the music-brain synchrony.
