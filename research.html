01 // SELECTED PROJECTS
EEG STUDY NEURAL SYNCHRONY AUDACITY TRF ANALYSIS

Effects of Musical Harmony on Neural Synchrony: An EEG Study

Using ten music tracks with almost the same tempo (2Hz), and modulated into three superposition versions (better and worse versions) by Audacity. Collecting subjects' tapping synchrony to the beats and EEG data, then using Temporal Response Function (TRF) to test the music-brain synchrony.

EEG STUDY PERCEPTUAL CURIOSITY KEYPRESS PARADIGM FUNDED PROJECT

EEG Signatures of Perceptual Curiosity: Interactions between Information Presentation and Sound Ratio

Funded by Chongqing Municipal Undergraduate Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program. This study employed a keypress–sound paradigm to investigate the differences and neural mechanisms of perceptual curiosity under information × valence conditions using EEG technology.
