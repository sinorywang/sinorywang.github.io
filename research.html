How Consonant and Dissonant Music affect Neural Synchrony

This study investigates the music-brain synchrony using ten house music tracks with a consistent BPM of 123. The tracks were modulated into better and worse superposition versions using Audacity. We collected subjects' tapping synchrony to the beats alongside EEG data, utilizing the Temporal Response Function (TRF) to analyze the neural entrainment.
