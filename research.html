Research

My research focuses on music cognition, mental health and neuroscience. I am interested in understanding how music influences emotional experience and cognitive processes using behavioral and physiological methods. Besides, I love to develop small web audio tools for auditory process, you can find them in my GitHub.

How Consanant and Dissant Music affect Neural synchrony: An EEG study

Using ten house music with almost same bpm(123), and modulate into better and worse superposition version by Audacity, collecting subjects tapping synchrony to the beats and EEG data, then using Temporal Response Function to test the music-brain synchrony.
